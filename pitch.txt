Game Idea
	A game where you dodge a great number of obsticles in your path until you reach the end.
	
Scenario and Character Descriptions
	The world will move up for a certain amount of time. If possible, multiple levels will be available.
	Objects and hazards will constantly spawn in and try to eliminate you.
	
	Characters can be anything.
	
Characters' Actions and goals
	The character's goal is to reach the end of the level.
	
Game Win/Lose Condition
	You win if you can acheive the goal.
	You lose if you touch a hazard or an object.
	
Progression of Levels
	As you move closer and closer to your objective, more hazard spawn in.
	
Sources of Inspiration
	Dodgeball
	Temple Run
	Geometry Dash
	The Impossible Game

	You are qualified.
